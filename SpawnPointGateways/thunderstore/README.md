# Spawn Point Gateways - The Bifrost Charm

A Valheim mod that lets you step back to any bed you have ever called home.
Every bed you mark as your spawn point is permanently remembered as a possible
destination — even after you switch to a different bed.

## How to use

1. Craft the Bifrost Charm at a Workbench (Level 2):
   - 10 Greydwarf Eyes
   - 1 Finewood
   - 20 Thistle
   - 10 Blueberries
2. Sleep on or "Set Spawn" at any bed. The position is recorded forever.
3. With at least 20 resin in your inventory, right-click the charm. The map
   opens. (Resin is checked but not yet consumed.)
4. Each recorded bed appears on the map as a small blue circle.
5. Click any blue circle to teleport — 20 resin are consumed and you arrive
   at that bed. Clicking elsewhere does nothing.

The Bifrost Charm itself is never consumed — only the resin is, and only on
a successful teleport.

Right-click anywhere on the map (off a marker) or press Esc to cancel without
spending any resin.

## Dependencies

- BepInEx 5.4.21+ (5.4.23.3 recommended)
- ValheimModding-Jotunn 2.29.0+

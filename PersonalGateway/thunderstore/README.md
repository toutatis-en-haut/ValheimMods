# Personal Gateway - The Bifrost Totem

Heimdall's Bifrost Totem: a Valheim mod that lets a single Viking step across
the world to any place already explored, fueled by sacrificing a trophy.

## How to use

1. Craft the Bifrost Totem at a Workbench (Level 2): 10 Greydwarf Eyes, 10 Thistle, 10 Dandelion, 10 Blueberries.
2. With at least one trophy in your inventory, right-click the totem to arm it.
3. Trophies in your inventory glow gold - right-click one to spend it.
4. The map opens with a light-blue range circle and a gold-star cursor.
5. Click anywhere inside the circle on terrain you have already explored to travel.

The Bifrost skill rises with each crossing; at max level (default 100) the circle disappears and you can step anywhere you have ever walked.

Right-click or Esc cancels an armed totem without spending the trophy.

## Dependencies

- BepInEx 5.4.21+ (5.4.23.3 recommended)
- ValheimModding-Jotunn 2.29.0+
